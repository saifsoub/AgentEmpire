# UAE Car Marketplace Research Guide

Use these source categories when building comparables:

1. Major UAE classifieds and auto marketplaces
2. UAE dealer inventory pages
3. Expat-focused UAE buy/sell communities
4. Auction/wholesale channels (when relevant)

## Comparable Quality Scoring

Score each comparable before using it in pricing:

- **A (high confidence):** same generation, close trim, mileage within ±15%, clear condition details
- **B (medium confidence):** same model/year range, limited condition detail
- **C (low confidence):** weak detail, likely distressed, or unclear authenticity

Use at least 5 comparables, with at least 3 from A/B tiers combined.

## Pricing Notes for UAE Context

- GCC-spec vs non-GCC spec can materially affect buyer demand.
- Full agency service history often supports premium pricing.
- Summer periods may reduce retail viewing activity for some segments.
- Popular commuter and family SUVs may preserve liquidity better than niche trims.

## Refresh Cadence

- Re-check top comparables every 72 hours for active negotiations.
- Rebuild full comp set every 7 days for unsold inventory.
